Who is SmokeyJoe?



We are a small group of NFT producers who are passionate about the NFT and metaverse space. SmokeyJoe is our character who is a character that we think society should take note of. A character who is cool headed, fashionable and chill. We truly believe in SmokeyJoe and its potential in the growing blockchain of Solana. There are 70 handdrawn SmokeyJoe's out of which 20 are released first and the rest 930 auto-generated SmokeyJoe's are auto-generated. The handdrawn Joe's are super rare and are the most valued. The one who buys a SmokeyJoe owns the collectible and has the right to do whatever he/she chooses. When he/she sells the NFT, complete ownership and rights is transferred to the new owner and the seller loses everything related to the sold NFT, including buyer community access.
